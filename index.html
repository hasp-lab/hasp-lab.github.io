Welcome

HASP is a research lab working at the intersection between Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, and Human-Computer Interaction. HASP is based at King's College London and is part of the cross-departmental KCL Cybersecurity Centre, and the Security Hub and the Cybersecurity Group at the Department of Informatics.
